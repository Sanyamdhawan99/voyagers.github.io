What We Are

Voyagers is a non-profitable organisation (registered under Chandigarh Administration) comprising of students of Punjab Engineering College (PEC). The organisation aims at developing in its members the ability to work for themselves and the group professionally. The motto "Conquering Zenith" aims at achieving the greatest heights which is indeed the aim of the organization. Our basic motto is to provide a bundle of events to the youth for them to choose, flourish in whichever way they wish to along with playing their role for betterment of the society. These events pertain to awareness amongst the youth, and development of the city.

Our Endeavours

Every year our event is known to attract a whopping crowd from colleges and schools all across the tri-city. Our events are in collaboration with various NGOs registered under Chandigarh Administration. NGOs perform a variety of services and humanitarian functions; bring citizens’ concerns to governments, monitor policy and various other tasks that the government fails to carry out perfectly, or at all. Instead, we provide a “helping hand” to the nation and make the best use of its resources to ensure maximum development. NGOs often hold an interesting role in a nation’s political, economic or social activities, as well as assessing and addressing problems in both national and international issues, such as human, political and women’s rights, economic development, democratization, healthcare or the environment.

Our Objective

Voyagers is and has always been a platform for its members to achieve whatever they desire of as it provides the exposure required for adapting with the changing world. We aim to provide learning in fields like discipline to develop an ethical value in the youth. Endeavour is our step to create an environment where learning is through the medium of rejuvenating activities. So we as a part of a NPO, have pledged to help the people who are underprivileged, needy and specially abled to live a better lifestyle.

Our Activities

We remain engaged in a plethora of activities throughout the year. 

General Body Meetings

General Body Meetings(GBMs) are a meduim for overall development of out members. They are held regularly twice a month and various team as well as individual tasks.
Apart from these, minor meeting are organized every week to discuss the progress of the organization.

Designing

Members are given basic knowledge of Photoshop, Priemere Pro, After Effects so that they are able to design and edit posters and videos.
Members engage in making posters for the annual event, recruitments and facebook posts.

Marketing

Voyagers organizes a tricity level event every year. The main motive behind organizing this event is to do some social work. Along with this memebers are exposed to various skills like Marketing, Designing, Event handling, Management, etc.
